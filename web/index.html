ONLINE EXAMINATION






Online Examination Instructions
(a.) Negative marking: Online examination do not have negative marking. Each question carry 1 marks.
(b.) During the Exam: Students must complete the 20 multiple choice question exam within the 30 minute timeframe allotted for the exam
(c.) Filling Data: Students must fill all their record (Name, College Name , Email Id and Date of Birth) after pressing Contiune button at bottom.
(d.) During the Exam: The student may not use their textbook, course notes, or receive help from any source.
(e.) Authority of Invigilator: The invigilator is officially in control of the examination and should be obeyed in all matters relating to the examination.
(f.) Identification: Candidates must produce their identity documents and student identification cards
(g.) Cellular Phones, Pagers, File-O-Fax, and Diaries: Candidates may not take these objects into the examination room.
